Our Story

The name means something

Bastech was founded with a simple belief: people deserve software that works for them — not around them. The name itself is personal, rooted in meaning, and built to last.

The company started not with investors or a business plan, but with a frustration — the kind that comes from using tools that almost solve your problem, but never quite get there. Grip was the answer to that frustration.

We're a small, focused company building products for the everyday individual. No enterprise pitch decks. No buzzwords. Just software that makes life a little easier.
