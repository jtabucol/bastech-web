Built for how small lenders
actually work

Not a bank. Not a spreadsheet. Lunas is the middle ground — simple enough to use daily, powerful enough to run your whole lending operation.

Borrower Management
Add and manage all your borrowers in one place. Name, contact, address, and full loan history — always within reach.
Loan Tracking
Create loans with custom principal, interest rate, and terms. Each loan is tracked separately with its own complete history.
Auto-Generated Schedules
Payment schedules are created automatically when a loan is set up. Principal and interest calculated per period — no manual math needed.
Partial Payments
Record payments of any amount. Lunas tracks what was paid, what's still owed, and marks each period accordingly — no double-counting.
Late Fees & Waivers
Charge late fees when you choose. Waive them when circumstances call for it. Every decision is logged — who did it, when, and why.
Staff & Borrower Portals
Your staff sees everything. Borrowers only see their own loans and payment history. Separate views, one system, full control.

1. Who We Are

Bastech ("we," "us," or "our") is a digital product company based in the Philippines. We build web applications including Grip, a personal finance tracker (grip.bastech.app), and Lunas, a lending management tool (lunas.bastech.app). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect across our website and products, how we use it, and the choices you have. If you have any questions, email us at support@bastech.app.

2. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account on one of our products, we collect your name, email address, and a password (stored in hashed form by our authentication provider — we never see or store your plain-text password).

Information you enter into our products. Grip stores the financial records you choose to enter, such as accounts, transactions, payables, and budget goals. Lunas stores the business records a lender enters, such as borrower details, loans, and payment schedules. This data belongs to you. We do not use it for advertising and we do not sell it to anyone.

Contact form submissions. If you contact us through our website form, we receive the name, email address, and message you submit.

Technical basics. Like most websites, our hosting and database providers keep standard technical logs (such as IP address and browser type) for security and reliability. We do not run third-party advertising trackers on our site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to provide and improve our services: keeping your account working, showing you your own data, sending essential service emails (such as account invitations, payment confirmations, or important product updates), responding to support requests, and keeping our systems secure. We do not sell personal data, and we do not share it with third parties for their marketing.

4. Where Your Data Lives

Your data is stored with Supabase, our cloud database and authentication provider, hosted in the Asia-Pacific (Singapore) region. All connections to our products are encrypted in transit using HTTPS.

5. Payments

Paid subscriptions are processed by Lemon Squeezy, our payment provider. Your card or payment details are entered directly with Lemon Squeezy and are never stored on Bastech systems. We receive only what we need to activate your plan, such as your email and subscription status.

6. Service Providers We Rely On

We use a small set of trusted providers to run our services: Supabase (database and login), Vercel (website hosting), Lemon Squeezy (payments), Brevo (transactional email delivery), and Formspree (contact form). Each receives only the information needed to do its job. When we publish content to our own social media pages (such as our Facebook Page), no customer data is involved.

Lunas is a tool for lenders to manage their own lending records. If you are a lender using Lunas, you are responsible for ensuring you have the right to record your borrowers' information. If you are a borrower whose details were entered by a lender, contact that lender first for corrections; you may also reach us at support@bastech.app and we will help where we can.

We keep your data for as long as your account is active.
